\abstract{
In recent years, Rstudio and Github have been widely regarded as one of
the essential research tools for social science inquiries. Previous
studies have revealed their significant methodological impact on
disciplines such as psychology, linguistics, education and so forth (Shi
\&Jin, 2017; Lee 2018). This study is an attempt to solve all the
questions listed in Week5 Lab8, one of the most challenging issues in
the history of Fall 2018 EDLD 610. Through a collaborative approach to
lab 8, this study aims to show that Rstudio and Github are learnable,
and sufficient training is one of the most efficient methods in R
language acquisition.


}
